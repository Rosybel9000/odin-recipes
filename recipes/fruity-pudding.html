Fruity Pudding

Description

I used french vanilla pudding and a fruit cocktail called Very Cherry by Del Monte. It gave it extra yummy flavor. Very quick and easy!

I'm sorry....this may be quick, but it's just too "chemical". It tastes like something made in a laboratory, not a kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (15.25 ounce) can fruit cocktail, drained

Steps

  1. Prepare the pudding according to package directions; refrigerate until chilled.
  2. Fold in the whipped topping and fruit; chill and serve.
